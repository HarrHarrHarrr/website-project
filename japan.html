Japan

Japan is one of the most popular holiday destinations in Asia, and it is easy to see why. With its iconic cherry blossom season, rich culture and history, Japan has a lot to offer for any interested tourist.

As well as our hotels in Japan's major cities, we provide excursions around the smaller, rural towns of Japan for those interested in the areas outside of Tokyo, including to the iconic Mount Fuji.
